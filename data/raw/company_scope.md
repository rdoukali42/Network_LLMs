# Company Overview

## About the Company
This company is a specialized Information Technology (IT) firm composed of a compact, highly skilled team of 10 professionals. Each member contributes deep expertise in their respective domain, allowing the company to operate effectively with a small but comprehensive structure. The primary mission is to design, build, deploy, and maintain advanced digital systems, software products, and AI-driven solutions for businesses and organizations.

## Core Focus Areas
- **Product Development**: End-to-end software and digital product creation.
- **Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning**: Research, development, and integration of intelligent systems.
- **Cybersecurity**: Protection of systems and data through audits, ethical hacking, and security enhancements.
- **Web Development & UX/UI Design**: Modern, responsive interfaces and seamless user experiences.
- **DevOps & Infrastructure**: CI/CD, system automation, cloud deployment, and environment management.
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensuring the stability, reliability, and quality of released software.
- **Data Analysis**: Converting raw data into business intelligence using modern analytical tools.
- **Project & Strategic Management**: Delivering high-impact tech projects on schedule and within scope.

## Escalation and Referral Guidelines

When requests fall outside the company's scope, employees should:

1. **Politely Decline**: Explain that the request falls outside the company's area of expertise
2. **Provide General Guidance**: Offer general direction on who might be able to help
3. **Document**: Record the request type for potential future policy updates
4. **Refer Appropriately**: Direct to appropriate external service providers when possible

### Common Referral Suggestions
- **Legal Matters**: "Please consult with a qualified attorney"
- **Medical Issues**: "Please consult with your healthcare provider"
- **Financial Advice**: "Please consult with a financial advisor"
- **Hardware Repairs**: "Please contact the manufacturer or a certified repair service"
- **Personal Services**: "Please contact appropriate personal service providers"

## Final Note

All employees should stay focused on their core responsibilities and align their efforts with the company's mission and code of conduct. The company's expertise lies in technology, business operations, human resources, compliance, and organizational management. External or off-topic requests should be politely declined and redirected to the appropriate service providers.

This comprehensive scope ensures that our ticket system can accurately determine whether issues can be resolved within our company network or require external resources, while maintaining our focus on delivering excellence in our core competency areas.
