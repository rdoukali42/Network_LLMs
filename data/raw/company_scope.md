
# Company Overview

## About the Company
This company is a specialized Information Technology (IT) firm composed of a compact, highly skilled team of 10 professionals. Each member contributes deep expertise in their respective domain, allowing the company to operate effectively with a small but comprehensive structure. The primary mission is to design, build, deploy, and maintain advanced digital systems, software products, and AI-driven solutions for businesses and organizations.

## Core Focus Areas
- **Product Development**: End-to-end software and digital product creation.
- **Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning**: Research, development, and integration of intelligent systems.
- **Cybersecurity**: Protection of systems and data through audits, ethical hacking, and security enhancements.
- **Web Development & UX/UI Design**: Modern, responsive interfaces and seamless user experiences.
- **DevOps & Infrastructure**: CI/CD, system automation, cloud deployment, and environment management.
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensuring the stability, reliability, and quality of released software.
- **Data Analysis**: Converting raw data into business intelligence using modern analytical tools.
- **Project & Strategic Management**: Delivering high-impact tech projects on schedule and within scope.

## What Can Be Solved Inside the Company Network
Requests and problems that fall within the following scopes can be addressed:
- Software bugs, system errors, and performance issues
- Infrastructure deployment, automation, or pipeline problems
- Product development feature requests or design feedback
- AI model training, integration, and evaluation
- UI/UX design improvements and testing feedback
- Security audits, vulnerability reports, and penetration testing
- Sprint or project timeline adjustments
- Dashboard creation, data queries, and report generation
- Gifting between Employees
- Quantum computer Challenges

## What Is Outside the Company’s Scope
This company does **not** handle personal, non-technical, or hardware-related tasks unrelated to software and IT operations. Examples of requests that fall outside the supported domain:
- Fixing household appliances such as washing machines or refrigerators
- Performing physical repairs or maintenance of electronic devices
- Providing customer service support for third-party products
- Assisting with legal, medical, or non-technical advisory services

## Final Note
All employees should stay focused on their core responsibilities and align their efforts with the company’s mission. External or off-topic requests should be politely declined and redirected to the appropriate service providers.
